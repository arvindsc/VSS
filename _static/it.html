IT Services

Vigilant IT, a division of VSS India, is a company engaged in providing a wide range of contemporary IT solutions to enterprises to help them leverage the power of Information Technology to grow their businesses.

Vigilant IT has a dedicated team of qualified, trained and experienced IT Professionals and Consultants trained with International quality standards, tools and technology. The IT division specialises in a range of products and services customised as per the varying needs of different enterprises.

Services

  • ERP and Business Process Re engineering.
  • Software Development: Combining latest technology, Industry standard methodologies & experience.
  • IT Security: IT security policy, audit, implementation, risk analysis, vulnerability assessment, penetration testing, forensics, etc.
  • Back Office Services.
  • IT Infrastructure & Datacentre Services: Designing, implementing and managing end-to-end IT infrastructure for the businesses, including Data Centers, BPO's / Call Centres with converged IP solutions.

G4S IT solutions are client centric and emphasise the alignment of technology solutions with businesses requirements to deliver the best value from IT investments.
